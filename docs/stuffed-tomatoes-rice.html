Rice-Stuffed Tomatoes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 🍅 6 medium-large tomatoes
  • 🍚 9 tbsp arborio rice
  • 🧅 1/4 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 🧄 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 🌶️ Red pepper flakes to taste
  • 🫗 2–3 tbsp olive oil
  • 🌿 Few tbsp chopped parsley, oregano, or basil
  • 🍞 Handful of breadcrumbs (optional)
  • 🧂 Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C. Lightly oil a baking dish
  2. Cut tops off tomatoes 🍅, scoop out flesh and juice into a bowl. Salt cavities, drain upside down
  3. Pulse the scooped flesh into a coarse purée
  4. Heat olive oil 🫗, sauté onion 🧅, garlic 🧄, and pepper flakes 🌶️ for 2 min. Add rice 🍚, toast 3 min
  5. Add tomato purée, season with salt 🧂, cover and simmer 10–15 min until rice 🍚 is par-cooked. Stir in herbs 🌿
  6. Fill tomatoes 🍅 to 7/8 full. Top with breadcrumbs 🍞 or tomato lids. Bake uncovered 30 min

Notes

  • Leave room in the tomatoes for rice to finish expanding
