Cauliflower Velouté

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 🌼 1 large cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 🥔 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 🫗 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 🧅 1 onion, chopped
  • 🧄 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 🌱 1 litre vegetable broth
  • 🌿 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil 🫗 in a large pot. Sauté onion 🧅 and garlic 🧄 until soft, about 5 min
  2. Add cauliflower 🌼, potatoes 🥔, and broth 🌱. Bring to a boil, then simmer 20 min until tender
  3. Blend with an immersion blender until smooth. Stir in thyme 🌿, season with salt and pepper 🧂

Notes

  • Keeps in the fridge 5 days, freezes up to 3 months
  • For richer flavour, roast the cauliflower 🌼 before adding to the pot
  • Serve with crusty bread or a side salad
