Chinese Blistered Green Beans

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 🫘 400–500g green beans, trimmed and dried well
  • 🫒 2–3 tbsp neutral oil
  • 🧄 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 🫚 1–2 tsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 🥫 1–2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 🍬 1 tsp sugar
  • 🍶 1–2 tsp Chinese black vinegar (or balsamic)

Steps

  1. Dry the beans very well — critical for blistering
  2. Heat a wok or large frying pan over high heat, add oil 🫒
  3. Add beans 🫘, cook 8–10 min, turning often, until blistered and charred in spots
  4. Push beans aside, add garlic 🧄 + ginger 🫚, fry 30 sec
  5. Return beans to the pan
  6. Add soy sauce 🥫, sugar 🍬, and vinegar 🍶 — toss 1–2 min, serve

Notes

  • High heat is key — that's what creates the blistered texture
  • Don't overcrowd the pan, or they'll steam instead of blister
  • Works great as a side for fish or with rice
