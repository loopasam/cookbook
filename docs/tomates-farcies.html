Tomates Farcies

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 🍅 4 large tomatoes (or 8 small)
  • 🥩 500g sausage meat
  • 🧅 3 onions, finely chopped
  • 🧄 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 🌿 Fresh thyme + parsley
  • 🧈 Butter (a knob per tomato)
  • 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Mix half the onions 🧅 with sausage meat 🥩, garlic 🧄, parsley 🌿, salt and pepper 🧂
  3. Cut tops off tomatoes 🍅, scoop out flesh. Season insides with salt and pepper 🧂. Fill with the meat stuffing 🥩, replace lids
  4. Spread remaining onions 🧅 and tomato flesh in a baking dish. Place stuffed tomatoes 🍅 on top
  5. Sprinkle with thyme 🌿, add a knob of butter 🧈 on each. Bake 1 hour

Notes

  • Serve with steamed rice
