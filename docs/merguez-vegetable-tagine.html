Merguez Vegetable Tagine

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 🌭 450g merguez sausage
  • 🧅 2 sweet white onions, sliced
  • 🥕 250g carrots, peeled and sliced lengthwise
  • 🎃 1 small butternut squash, peeled and chopped
  • 🥒 250g zucchini, sliced lengthwise
  • 🧄 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 🫒 1 cup green olives, pitted
  • 🍋 1 lemon, quartered
  • 🫘 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 🍇 1/2 cup golden raisins + 1/2 cup dried apricots, chopped
  • 🌿 1 cup cilantro, finely chopped
  • 🟤 2 tsp cinnamon + 1 tsp coriander + 2 tsp paprika + 1 tsp turmeric + 3 tsp cumin
  • 🫗 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 🍗 3 cups chicken stock
  • 🌾 1 tbsp flour

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil 🫗 in a large pot. Add the spice mix 🟤 and stir briefly
  2. Add lemon 🍋, onions 🧅, garlic 🧄, and olives 🫒. Cook until soft, about 15 min
  3. Brown the merguez 🌭 in a skillet, reserve the rendered oil. Set aside
  4. Stir flour 🌾 into the pot, add chicken stock 🍗 and simmer 5 min
  5. Add merguez 🌭 with its oil, carrots 🥕, squash 🎃, zucchini 🥒, chickpeas 🫘, raisins and apricots 🍇. Cook 15 min until tender
  6. Garnish with cilantro 🌿. Serve with couscous

Notes

  • Use a tagine if available, otherwise a dutch oven works great
  • Stir turmeric into the couscous before covering for a yellow hue
