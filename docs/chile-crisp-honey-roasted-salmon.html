Chile Crisp Honey Roasted Salmon

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 🐟 900g center-cut salmon fillet (skin on or off)
  • 🧂 Salt and pepper
  • 🥚 60g mayonnaise
  • 🌶️ 2 tbsp chile crisp, plus more to taste
  • 🍯 2 tbsp honey
  • 🍋 Zest of 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 190°C with a rack in the middle
  2. Put the salmon 🐟 in a snug baking dish, season both sides with salt and pepper 🧂, and place it skin-side down if using skin-on fish
  3. Mix mayonnaise 🥚, chile crisp 🌶️, honey 🍯, and lemon zest 🍋. Taste and adjust with more chile crisp for heat or honey for sweetness
  4. Spread the sauce evenly over the salmon 🐟
  5. Roast 15–25 min, depending on thickness, until the thickest part flakes easily and the topping bubbles at the edges
  6. Spoon any glaze from the dish back over the salmon before serving

Notes

  • Adapted from Naz Deravian, NYT Cooking
  • Serve with rice, roasted potatoes, or a peppery arugula salad
