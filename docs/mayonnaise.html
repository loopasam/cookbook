Mayonnaise

Servings: 1 cup

Ingredients

  • 🥚 1 large egg yolk
  • 🟡 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 🍋 1 lemon, juiced
  • 🫗 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 🧂 Salt to taste
  • 🌑 Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine egg yolk 🥚, dijon mustard 🟡, and lemon juice 🍋 in a blender. Blend until mixed
  2. With the blender on low, slowly drizzle in the oil 🫗 — go gradually to emulsify properly
  3. Continue adding oil 🫗 until fully incorporated and thick
  4. Season with salt 🧂 and pepper 🌑. Refrigerate

Notes

  • All ingredients should be at room temperature
  • If too thick, whisk in a teaspoon of water
