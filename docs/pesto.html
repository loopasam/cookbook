Pesto

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 🌿 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 🧀 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 🌰 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 🧄 3 garlic cloves
  • 🧂 1/4 tsp salt
  • 🌑 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 🫗 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Place basil 🌿, parmesan 🧀, pine nuts 🌰, garlic 🧄, salt 🧂, and pepper 🌑 in a food processor
  2. Slowly drizzle in olive oil 🫗 while the processor is running
  3. Pulse until smooth

Notes

  • Swap pine nuts 🌰 for walnuts, almonds, or sunflower seeds
  • Swap basil 🌿 for spinach, arugula, radish leaves, or kale
  • Stores in the fridge up to a week, freezes up to 3 months
