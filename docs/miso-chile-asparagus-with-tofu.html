Miso-Chile Asparagus With Tofu

Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 🧊 1 block (14–16 oz) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 🌿 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 🫗 3 tbsp neutral oil (grapeseed or peanut)
  • 🧂 Salt
  • 🧄 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 🌶️ ½ tsp red-pepper flakes, plus more for serving
  • 🫘 2 tbsp white miso
  • 🍶 1 tbsp sake (or white wine)
  • 🍚 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 🍯 1 tbsp mirin
  • 🫙 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 🧅 Sliced scallions, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat broiler with rack 6 inches from heat. Cube the tofu 🧊 and pat dry. Toss tofu 🧊 and asparagus 🌿 on a foil-lined sheet pan with 2 tbsp oil 🫗, season with salt 🧂, and spread in one layer
  2. Broil until lightly browned, 3–6 min. Flip and broil until asparagus 🌿 is tender and tofu 🧊 is charred in spots, 3–7 min more
  3. Meanwhile, heat remaining 1 tbsp oil 🫗 in a skillet over medium. Cook garlic 🧄 and red-pepper flakes 🌶️ until fragrant, 30–60 sec. Remove from heat and whisk in miso 🫘, sake 🍶, vinegar 🍚, mirin 🍯, and sesame oil 🫙
  4. Slather miso sauce over asparagus 🌿 and tofu 🧊, return to broiler 30 sec–2 min until bubbling. Garnish with scallions 🧅 and extra red-pepper flakes 🌶️

Notes

  • Serve over rice or noodles for a complete meal
