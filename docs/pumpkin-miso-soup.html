Pumpkin Miso Soup

Servings: 4

Prep: Roasted Pumpkin

  • 🎃 1 medium pumpkin
  • 🫗 2 tbsp olive oil
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Cut the pumpkin 🎃 in half, scoop out seeds, leave skin on
  2. Spread olive oil 🫗 on the pumpkin 🎃 halves, place on a baking sheet
  3. Roast 25–30 min until tender and edges are slightly browned
  4. Cool 10 min, then scoop out flesh and discard skin

Ingredients

  • 🏺 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 🧅 1 medium yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 🧄 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 💛 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 🌱 1 litre vegetable broth
  • 🎃 400g pumpkin purée (from prep above)
  • 🥄 3 tbsp white miso paste
  • 🍶 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste
  • 🟢 Sliced green onions or nori strips (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil 🏺 in a large pot. Sauté onion 🧅, garlic 🧄, and ginger 💛 until translucent, about 5 min
  2. Stir in pumpkin purée 🎃 and broth 🌱. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer
  3. Add soy sauce 🍶 and miso paste 🥄 — do not boil after adding miso
  4. Blend with an immersion blender until smooth. Simmer 20 min
  5. Season with salt and pepper 🧂. Serve garnished with green onions 🟢

Notes

  • Stores in the fridge up to a week, freezes well
  • Substitute butternut squash for pumpkin 🎃
  • Add a splash of coconut milk for creamier texture
