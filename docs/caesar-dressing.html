Caesar Dressing

Servings: 1 cup

Prep: Mayonnaise

  • 🥚 1 egg yolk
  • 🫗 200ml sunflower oil
  • 🍋 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 🧂 Pinch of salt
  1. Whisk the egg yolk 🥚 with lemon juice 🍋 and salt 🧂
  2. Slowly drizzle in the oil 🫗 while whisking until emulsified

Ingredients

  • 🥣 1 cup mayonnaise (from prep above)
  • 🧀 1/2 cup parmigiano reggiano, grated
  • 🟤 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 🐟 4 anchovies
  • 🧄 2 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Combine mayonnaise 🥣, parmigiano 🧀, Worcestershire sauce 🟤, anchovies 🐟, and garlic 🧄 in a food processor. Blend until smooth
  2. Transfer to a container and refrigerate

Notes

  • Produces a thicker version of the classic dressing
  • All ingredients should be at room temperature for best results
  • For Caesar salad: toss with romaine lettuce and croutons, top with more parmigiano 🧀
