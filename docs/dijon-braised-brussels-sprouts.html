Dijon-Braised Brussels Sprouts

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 🥬 450g brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
  • 🧈 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 🫗 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 🧅 2–3 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 🍷 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 🍗 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • 🥛 2 tbsp heavy cream
  • 🟡 1 tbsp dijon mustard (or more to taste)
  • 🌿 2 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, chopped (optional)
  • 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter 🧈 and oil 🫗 in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Place sprouts 🥬 cut-side down in one layer. Cook without turning until golden, about 5 min
  3. Add shallots 🧅, wine 🍷, and broth 🍗. Bring to a simmer, cover, cook 15–20 min until tender
  4. Remove sprouts 🥬. Add cream 🥛, simmer 2–3 min until thickened. Whisk in mustard 🟡
  5. Season with salt and pepper 🧂. Pour sauce over sprouts 🥬, garnish with parsley 🌿

Notes

  • Brown sprouts in batches if they don't fit in one layer
