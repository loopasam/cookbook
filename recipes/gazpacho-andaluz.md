---
title: Gazpacho Andaluz
category: starters
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🍅 1 kg ripe tomatoes, quartered
- 🥒 1 cucumber, peeled and roughly chopped
- 🫑 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 🧅 1 small red onion, roughly chopped
- 🧄 1 garlic clove, peeled
- 🍞 50 g stale bread, crusts removed, soaked in a little water
- 🫗 3 tbsp good olive oil
- 🍶 2 tbsp sherry vinegar (or red wine vinegar)
- 💧 150 ml cold water
- 🧂 Salt to taste

## Steps
1. Throw everything in a blender — tomatoes 🍅, cucumber 🥒, pepper 🫑, onion 🧅, garlic 🧄, soaked bread 🍞, olive oil 🫗, vinegar 🍶, water 💧, and a good pinch of salt 🧂
2. Blitz until completely smooth, 1–2 minutes
3. Strain through a fine sieve if you want it silky; skip if you like it rustic
4. Taste and adjust: more vinegar 🍶 for brightness, more salt 🧂, maybe a splash more water 💧 if thick
5. Chill at least 2 hours (or overnight). Serve very cold

## Garnish ideas
- Diced cucumber 🥒, bell pepper 🫑, and red onion 🧅
- Hard-boiled egg 🥚, chopped
- Croutons 🍞 or a drizzle of olive oil 🫗

## Notes
- Add half a roasted red pepper for sweetness and depth
- A pinch of smoked paprika gives it a subtle warmth
