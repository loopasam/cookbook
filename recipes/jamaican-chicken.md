---
title: Jamaican Chicken
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🍗 600g chicken thighs
- 🌶️ 2.5 tbsp Karjos Easispice Chicken Seasoning (or Jamaican jerk spice mix)
- 🫒 2 tbsp olive oil
- 🍋 2 tbsp lime juice
- 🧅 1 onion, sliced (optional)
- 💧 ¼ cup water

## Steps
1. Rub chicken thighs 🍗 with spice mix 🌶️
2. Set Instant Pot to Sauté, heat olive oil 🫒
3. Sear thighs 3 min per side until browned, remove
4. Add onion 🧅, sauté 2 min
5. Deglaze with lime juice 🍋, scrape the bottom
6. Add water 💧, return chicken to pot
7. Seal, cook on High Pressure 10 min, natural release 10 min
8. Rest 2 min, slice or serve whole

## Notes
- Don't add extra salt — the spice mix is already salted
- Optional: finish under the broiler 2–3 min for crispy skin
- Serve with coconut rice or cabbage slaw
