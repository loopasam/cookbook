---
title: Jamaican Chicken
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🍗 600g chicken breasts
- 🌶️ 3 tbsp Jamaican jerk spice mix
- 🫒 2 tbsp olive oil
- 🧄 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 🍋 2 tbsp lime juice
- 🧂 1 tsp salt
- ⬛ ½ tsp black pepper
- 🧅 1 onion, sliced (optional)

## Steps
1. Rub chicken breasts 🍗 with Jamaican jerk spice 🌶️, salt 🧂, and pepper ⬛
2. Set Instant Pot to Sauté, heat olive oil 🫒
3. Sear chicken 2–3 min per side until browned, remove
4. Add garlic 🧄 and onion 🧅, sauté 2 min
5. Deglaze with lime juice 🍋, scrape the bottom
6. Return chicken to pot, add ¼ cup water
7. Seal, cook on High Pressure 8 min, natural release 5 min
8. Rest 2 min, slice against the grain

## Notes
- Shortcut: use store-bought jerk spice mix (Walkerswood, Grace, or similar)
- Upgrade: finish under the broiler 2–3 min for charred edges
- Serve with coconut rice or cabbage slaw
