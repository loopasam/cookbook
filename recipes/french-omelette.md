---
title: French Omelette
category: mains
servings: 1
---

## Ingredients
- 🥚 3 eggs
- 🧈 15g butter
- 🧂 Salt
- 🌶️ White pepper
- 🌿 Fresh chives

## Steps
1. Crack the eggs 🥚 into a bowl, season with salt 🧂 and white pepper 🌶️
2. Beat until just combined
3. Melt the butter 🧈 in a non-stick pan over medium heat
4. Pour in the eggs 🥚 and stir gently with a fork
5. When barely set, fold the omelette and slide onto a plate
6. Garnish with fresh chives 🌿
