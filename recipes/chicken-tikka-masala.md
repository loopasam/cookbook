---
title: Chicken Tikka Masala
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients

- 600g chicken thighs, cut in chunks
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 1 can coconut cream
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Rice to serve

## Method

1. Sauté onion in Instant Pot on Sauté mode
2. Add garlic and all spices, stir 1 min
3. Add chicken pieces, seal IP, pressure cook 8 min
4. Quick release → stir in crushed tomatoes
5. Cook on Sauté 5 min
6. Stir in coconut cream → serve with rice
