---
title: Chicken Tikka Masala
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients

- 900g chicken thighs, cut in chunks
- 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
- 1 can coconut cream
- 1 onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 200ml chicken broth
- Rice to serve

## Method

1. Sauté onion in Instant Pot on Sauté mode
2. Add garlic and all spices, stir 1 min
3. Add chicken pieces, tomatoes, and broth — mix everything together
4. Seal IP, pressure cook 9 min
5. Quick release → stir in coconut cream
6. Cook on Sauté 3-5 min to thicken slightly
7. Serve with rice

## Enhancements

- Naan bread alongside rice
- Add cashews or almonds for crunch
- Finish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lemon
