---
title: Lemon Vinaigrette
category: basics
servings: 1 cup
---

## Ingredients
- 🫗 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 🍇 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
- 🟡 2 tsp dijon mustard
- 🍋 1 lemon, zest and juice
- 🧄 1 tsp garlic powder
- 🍯 1 tbsp honey
- 🧂 1 tsp salt
- 🌑 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 🌿 2 tsp dried oregano

## Steps
1. Whisk together olive oil 🫗, balsamic vinegar 🍇, dijon mustard 🟡, lemon zest and juice 🍋, garlic powder 🧄, honey 🍯, salt 🧂, pepper 🌑, and oregano 🌿
2. Shake well before using if separated

## Notes
- Stores in the fridge for up to 2 weeks
- Use a squeeze bottle for easy dispensing
