---
title: Pesto Beans
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🫒 8 tbsp olive oil
- 🌰 ¼ cup pine nuts or sliced almonds
- 🟡 ½ tsp ground turmeric
- 🧅 3 large shallots, thinly sliced
- 🧄 5 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 🫘 2 (400g) cans cannellini beans, drained
- 🍲 1 cup vegetable or chicken stock
- 🧀 1 cup finely grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
- 🌿 1½ cups tightly packed basil leaves
- 🍋 1 lemon, cut into wedges

## Steps
1. Heat 6 tbsp olive oil 🫒 over medium-low. Add the pine nuts 🌰 and stir until golden, ~5 min. Stir in turmeric 🟡, season with salt & pepper, transfer nuts and oil to a bowl.
2. Heat remaining 2 tbsp olive oil 🫒 over medium-high. Add shallots 🧅 with a pinch of salt, cook ~3 min. Stir in garlic 🧄 for 1 min, then add beans 🫘 and stock 🍲. Bring to a simmer, reduce to low.
3. Sprinkle in Parmesan 🧀 in batches, stirring vigorously until creamy. Season with salt, turn off heat. Tear basil 🌿 and stir into the hot beans.
4. Serve drizzled with the sizzled nut oil, a squeeze of lemon 🍋, black pepper and extra Parmesan 🧀.
