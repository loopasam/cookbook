title: Instant Pot Dal
category: mains
servings: 6
---

## Ingredients
- 🔴 500g red lentils, rinsed
- 🧅 2 onions, diced
- 🧄 8 cloves garlic, minced
- 🫚 2 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 🍅 2 cans (800ml) diced tomatoes
- 🌿 2 tsp turmeric
- 🌿 2 tsp cumin
- 🌿 2 tsp garam masala
- 🌶️ 1 tsp chili powder
- 💧 1.2L water or broth
- 🧂 2 tsp salt
- 🧈 80g butter or ghee

## Steps
1. Sauté mode: melt butter 🧈, add onion 🧅. Cook 3–4 min until soft.
2. Add garlic 🧄, ginger 🫚, all spices 🌿🌶️. Stir 1 min until fragrant.
3. Add lentils 🔴, tomatoes 🍅, water 💧, salt 🧂. Stir, scrape any bits from the bottom.
4. Pressure cook 10 min high, natural release 10 min, then quick release.
5. Stir well — it'll thicken as it cools. Add hot water if too thick.
6. Serve with rice 🍚 or naan. Yogurt on top if you have it.

## Notes
- Simple weeknight dinner — skip the tadka and bloom the spices right in the pot
- Good with a dollop of yogurt and fresh cilantro
- Freezes well
