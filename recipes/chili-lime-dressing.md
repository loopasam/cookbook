---
title: Chili Lime Dressing
category: basics
servings: 1 cup
---

## Ingredients
- 🍈 2 limes, zest and juice
- 🍇 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 🍶 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 🍯 1 tbsp honey
- 🫗 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 🧄 1 tsp garlic powder
- 🌶️ 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 🌱 1 tsp ground cumin
- 🧂 1/2 tsp salt

## Steps
1. Whisk together lime zest and juice 🍈, red wine vinegar 🍇, soy sauce 🍶, honey 🍯, olive oil 🫗, garlic powder 🧄, red pepper flakes 🌶️, cumin 🌱, and salt 🧂
2. Shake well before using if separated

## Notes
- Stores in the fridge for up to 2 weeks
- Use a squeeze bottle for easy dispensing
