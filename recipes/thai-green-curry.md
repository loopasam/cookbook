---
title: Thai Green Curry
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🍗 500g chicken thighs
- 🟢 3 tbsp green curry paste
- 🥥 400ml coconut milk
- 🍆 1 eggplant
- 🫑 1 red bell pepper
- 🌿 Thai basil leaves
- 🐟 2 tbsp fish sauce

## Steps
1. Cut the chicken 🍗 into bite-size pieces
2. Fry the green curry paste 🟢 in a wok for 1 minute
3. Add coconut milk 🥥 and bring to a simmer
4. Add the chicken 🍗 and cook 10 minutes
5. Add the eggplant 🍆 and bell pepper 🫑, cook 5 more minutes
6. Season with fish sauce 🐟 and top with Thai basil 🌿
