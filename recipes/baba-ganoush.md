---
title: Baba Ganoush
category: starters
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🍆 2 medium eggplants
- 🥄 3 tbsp tahini
- 🍋 1 lemon, juiced
- 🧄 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 🥣 1 tbsp Greek yoghurt
- 🧂 1/4 tsp salt
- 🌑 1/8 tsp pepper
- 🌿 1 tsp sumac
- 🫗 2 tbsp olive oil
- 🌶️ 1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Poke the eggplants 🍆 with a fork, place on a baking sheet and roast 30–40 min until charred and soft
2. Let the eggplants 🍆 cool 15 min, then peel off and discard the skin
3. Place the eggplant 🍆 flesh in a food processor with tahini 🥄, lemon juice 🍋, garlic 🧄, yoghurt 🥣, salt 🧂, pepper 🌑, sumac 🌿, and red pepper flakes 🌶️. Pulse until smooth
4. Transfer to a serving dish, drizzle with olive oil 🫗. Serve with pita or fresh vegetables

## Notes
- Stores in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week
