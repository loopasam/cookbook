---
title: Banana Bread
servings: 8
---

## Ingredients
- 🍌 3 ripe bananas
- 🧈 100g melted butter
- 🥚 2 eggs
- 🍚 200g flour
- 🍬 150g sugar
- 🧂 1 tsp baking soda

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 175°C
2. Mash the bananas 🍌 in a bowl
3. Mix in the melted butter 🧈 and eggs 🥚
4. Stir in the sugar 🍬, flour 🍚, and baking soda 🧂
5. Pour into a greased loaf pan and bake 60 minutes

## Notes
- The riper the bananas 🍌, the better
