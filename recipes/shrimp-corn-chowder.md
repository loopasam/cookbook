---
title: Shrimp & Corn Chowder
category: starters
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🥓 4 slices bacon, diced
- 🦐 450g medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 🧄 3 garlic cloves, diced
- 🧅 1 onion, diced
- 🌶️ 2 tsp smoked paprika + 1/2 tsp oregano + 1/2 tsp basil
- 🍗 4 cups chicken stock
- 🌽 2 cups corn kernels
- 🥛 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 🌿 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Cook bacon 🥓 in a large pot until crispy. Set aside, keep 1 tbsp fat
2. Sear shrimp 🦐 in the pot until pink, about 2 min. Set aside
3. Sauté garlic 🧄 and onion 🧅 until translucent. Add spices 🌶️, season with salt and pepper 🧂
4. Add chicken stock 🍗 and corn 🌽. Boil, then simmer 15 min until thickened
5. Stir in cream 🥛 and blend with an immersion blender
6. Serve topped with shrimp 🦐, bacon 🥓, and parsley 🌿

## Notes
- Roasted corn adds extra smoky sweetness
