---
title: Red Lentil Soup (Instant Pot)
category: starters
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🔴 1 1/2 cups red lentils, rinsed
- 🧅 1 onion, diced
- 🥕 2 carrots, diced
- 🧄 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 🫗 2 tbsp olive oil
- 🌿 1 tsp cumin + 1 tsp turmeric + 1/2 tsp paprika
- 🍅 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
- 🌱 4 cups vegetable broth
- 🍋 1 lemon, juiced
- 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Set Instant Pot to sauté. Heat olive oil 🫗, cook onion 🧅, carrots 🥕, and garlic 🧄 for 3 min
2. Add spices 🌿 and stir 30 seconds until fragrant
3. Add lentils 🔴, tomatoes 🍅, and broth 🌱. Stir to combine
4. Seal and pressure cook on high for 10 min. Natural release 10 min
5. Blend with an immersion blender until smooth. Stir in lemon juice 🍋, season with salt and pepper 🧂

## Notes
- Top with a swirl of olive oil and fresh cilantro
- Freezes well for up to 3 months
