---
title: Shaved Asparagus Pizza
category: mains
servings: 1 pizza (12")
---

## Ingredients
- 🟢 500g asparagus
- 🧀 ¼ cup grated Parmesan
- 🧀 225g mozzarella, shredded or cubed
- 🫒 2 tsp olive oil
- 🧂 ½ tsp coarse salt
- ⬛ Black pepper
- 🌿 2 scallions, thinly sliced
- 🍕 1 premade pizza dough (thin, 12")

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to max (250°C / 500°F). Pizza stone in if you have one.
2. Shave asparagus — hold by the tough end, use a Y-peeler to peel long thin ribbons from base to tip. Discard tough ends.
3. Toss shavings with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
4. Stretch out dough on a floured surface.
5. Top with mozzarella, then pile on asparagus shavings and Parmesan.
6. Scatter scallion on top.
7. Bake 8–10 min until crust is golden and charred at edges.

## Notes
- Use a mandoline if no Y-peeler — same effect, watch your fingers
- Upgrade: drizzle with lemon juice or add prosciutto ribbons before baking
