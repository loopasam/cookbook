---
title: Crêpes
category: desserts
servings: 15
---

## Ingredients
- 🌾 300g flour
- 🥚 3 eggs
- 🥛 75cl milk
- 🫗 3 tbsp oil

## Steps
1. Mix flour 🌾, eggs 🥚, milk 🥛, and oil 🫗 until smooth
2. Cook one at a time in a non-stick crêpe pan over medium heat
3. Flip when edges lift and underside is golden, about 1 min per side

## Notes
- Rub pan with an oiled paper towel if not using non-stick
- Fill with sugar & lemon, Nutella, or ham & cheese
