---
title: Pancakes
category: desserts
servings: 8-10 pancakes
---

## Ingredients
- 🌾 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 🥄 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 🍚 1 tbsp white sugar
- 🧂 1/4 tsp salt
- 🥛 1 1/4 cups milk
- 🧈 3 tbsp butter, melted
- 🥚 1 egg

## Steps
1. Whisk together flour 🌾, baking powder 🥄, sugar 🍚, and salt 🧂
2. Add milk 🥛, melted butter 🧈, and egg 🥚. Whisk until smooth
3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
4. Pour about 1/4 cup batter per pancake. Brown 2–3 min on each side. Serve hot

## Notes
- Add sliced fruit, vanilla extract, or cocoa powder to the batter for variations
