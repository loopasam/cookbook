---
title: Gratin Dauphinois
category: sides
servings: 6
---

## Ingredients
- 🥔 1.5kg potatoes
- 🧄 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 🥛 1l milk
- 🫙 30cl cream
- 🧈 100g butter
- 🫚 pinch of nutmeg
- 🧂 salt and pepper

## Steps
1. Peel, wash and thinly slice the potatoes 🥔 (don't rinse after slicing — the starch is needed)
2. Bring the milk 🥛, garlic 🧄, salt 🧂 and nutmeg 🫚 to a boil, add the potatoes 🥔 and simmer 10–15 min
3. Preheat oven to 180°C and butter a gratin dish with some of the butter 🧈
4. Transfer the drained potatoes 🥔 to the dish, pour over the cream 🫙 and dot with remaining butter 🧈
5. Bake for 50 min to 1 hour until golden

## Notes
- Use the leftover milk from step 2 for soup or mashed potatoes
