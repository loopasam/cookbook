---
title: Tzatziki
category: starters
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🥣 1 cup Greek yoghurt
- 🥒 1 cucumber, peeled and grated
- 🧄 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 🌿 1 tbsp fresh dill or mint, chopped
- 🫗 1 tbsp olive oil
- 🍋 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Grate the cucumber 🥒, place in a sieve and press out as much liquid as possible. Let drain a few minutes
2. Combine drained cucumber 🥒, yoghurt 🥣, garlic 🧄, olive oil 🫗, lemon juice 🍋, and dill 🌿 in a bowl. Mix well
3. Season with salt and pepper 🧂. Adjust garlic 🧄 and herbs 🌿 to taste
4. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving

## Notes
- Drain the cucumber 🥒 thoroughly to avoid watery tzatziki
- Stores in the fridge for a few days
