---
title: Greek Salad
category: salads
servings: 2
---

## Ingredients
- 🍅 3 tomatoes
- 🥒 1 cucumber
- 🫒 100g kalamata olives
- 🧀 150g feta cheese
- 🧅 1 red onion
- 🫗 Olive oil
- 🌿 Dried oregano

## Steps
1. Cut the tomatoes 🍅 and cucumber 🥒 into chunks
2. Slice the red onion 🧅 into thin rings
3. Combine tomatoes 🍅, cucumber 🥒, onion 🧅, and olives 🫒 in a bowl
4. Place the feta 🧀 on top in a single block
5. Drizzle with olive oil 🫗 and sprinkle oregano 🌿
