---
title: Lomo Saltado
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🥩 500g beef sirloin, cut into strips
- 🧅 1 red onion, sliced into wedges
- 🍅 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 🌶️ 1 ají amarillo (or jalapeño), sliced
- 🧄 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 🍶 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 🍇 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 🫗 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 🥔 2 large potatoes, cut into fries
- 🌿 Fresh cilantro, chopped

## Steps
1. Fry the potatoes 🥔 until golden and crispy. Set aside
2. Heat oil 🫗 in a wok over high heat. Sear the beef 🥩 in batches until browned. Set aside
3. In the same wok, stir-fry onion 🧅, garlic 🧄, and ají amarillo 🌶️ for 1 min
4. Add tomatoes 🍅, soy sauce 🍶, and vinegar 🍇. Toss for 30 seconds
5. Return the beef 🥩, add fries 🥔, and toss together. Garnish with cilantro 🌿

## Notes
- Serve with steamed white rice
- The wok must be very hot for proper searing
