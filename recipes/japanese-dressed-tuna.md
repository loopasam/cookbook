---
title: Japanese Dressed Tuna
category: mains
servings: 2
---

## Ingredients
- 🐟 1 can tuna in oil (~150–160 g), drained well
- 🍶 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 🫘 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
- 🍚 1 tsp rice vinegar (or mirin for a sweeter touch)
- 🌱 1 scallion, thinly sliced (optional)
- 🌶️ Pinch of chili flakes or shichimi togarashi (optional)
- 🍚 Cooked rice, to serve

## Steps
1. Drain the tuna 🐟 well and flake it into a bowl
2. Add soy sauce 🍶, sesame oil 🫘, and rice vinegar. Mix gently
3. Fold in scallion 🌱 and chili 🌶️ if using
4. Serve over warm rice 🍚, on toast, or in a salad

## Notes
- Grated ginger and toasted sesame seeds are great upgrades
- A soft-boiled egg on top turns it into a full meal
- Also works cold as a rice-bowl topping for lunch boxes
