---
title: Easy Pad Thai
category: mains
servings: 2
---

## Ingredients
- 🍜 200g flat rice noodles
- 🍤 200g shrimp, peeled
- 🥚 2 eggs
- 🫘 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 🍯 1 tbsp tamarind paste
- 🍚 1 tbsp sugar
- 🧄 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 🥜 30g roasted peanuts, crushed
- 🌱 2 spring onions, sliced
- 🍋 1 lime, cut into wedges

## Steps
1. Soak the rice noodles 🍜 in warm water for 20 min, then drain
2. Mix the fish sauce 🫘, tamarind paste 🍯, and sugar 🍚 in a small bowl
3. Stir-fry the garlic 🧄 for 30 seconds, add the shrimp 🍤 and cook until pink
4. Push aside and scramble the eggs 🥚 in the same pan
5. Add the noodles 🍜 and sauce, toss until well coated
6. Serve topped with peanuts 🥜, spring onions 🌱, and lime 🍋 wedges

## Notes
- Swap shrimp for tofu or chicken if preferred
- Add bean sprouts and chili flakes for extra texture and heat
