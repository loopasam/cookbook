---
title: Sesame Ginger Dressing
category: basics
servings: 1 cup
---

## Ingredients
- 🫗 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 🏺 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
- 🍶 1/4 cup rice vinegar + 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 🧄 1 tsp garlic powder
- 🍯 1 tbsp honey
- 🌱 2 tsp ground ginger

## Steps
1. Whisk together olive oil 🫗, sesame oil 🏺, rice vinegar and soy sauce 🍶, garlic powder 🧄, honey 🍯, and ginger 🌱
2. Shake well before using if separated

## Notes
- Stores in the fridge for up to 2 weeks
- Use a squeeze bottle for easy dispensing
