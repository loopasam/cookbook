---
title: Lukewarm Vietnamese Rice Noodles
category: mains
servings: 2
---

## Ingredients
- 🍜 200g rice vermicelli
- 🥕 1 carrot, julienned
- 🥒 ½ cucumber, julienned
- 🌿 1 handful mint leaves
- 🌿 1 handful cilantro
- 🥜 2 tbsp roasted peanuts, crushed
- 🧄 1 clove garlic, minced
- 🌶️ 1 red chili, sliced (optional)
- 🍋 2 tbsp lime juice
- 🐟 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 🍚 1 tsp sugar
- 🫒 1 tbsp vegetable oil

## Steps
1. Cook rice vermicelli 🍜 per package, drain, rinse under cold water, leave lukewarm
2. Whisk lime juice 🍋, fish sauce 🐟, sugar 🍚, and garlic 🧄 into a dressing
3. Toss noodles with carrot 🥕, cucumber 🥒, mint 🌿, and cilantro 🌿
4. Drizzle dressing over, toss gently
5. Top with peanuts 🥜 and chili 🌶️, serve lukewarm

## Notes
- Shortcut: use pre-shredded slaw mix instead of julienned veg
- Upgrade: add grilled shrimp or sliced leftover chicken
