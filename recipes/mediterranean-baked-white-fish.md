---
title: Mediterranean Baked White Fish
category: mains
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🐟 4 white fish fillets (cod, halibut, or sea bass)
- 🫗 2 tbsp olive oil
- 🍋 1 lemon, juiced and zested
- 🧄 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 🌿 2 tsp dried oregano + 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 🧅 1 red onion, minced
- 🍅 300g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 🫒 100g pitted olives (kalamata, green, or mix)
- 🧂 Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps
1. Preheat oven to 200°C
2. Mix olive oil 🫗, lemon juice 🍋, garlic 🧄, herbs 🌿, salt and pepper 🧂
3. Place fish fillets 🐟 in a baking dish. Pour the herb-lemon mixture over them, coating evenly
4. Scatter cherry tomatoes 🍅, olives 🫒, and red onion 🧅 around and on top of the fish 🐟
5. Bake 15–20 min until the fish 🐟 flakes easily with a fork

## Notes
- Serve with rice, couscous, or a green salad
- Add capers for extra Mediterranean flavour
