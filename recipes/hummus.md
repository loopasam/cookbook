---
title: Hummus
category: starters
servings: 4
---

## Ingredients
- 🥫 400g can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 🍋 2 lemons, juiced
- 🧄 1 garlic clove
- 🧂 1/2 tsp fine sea salt
- 🥄 1/2 cup tahini
- 💧 2 tbsp water
- 🌿 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 🫗 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

## Steps
1. Place chickpeas 🥫, lemon juice 🍋, garlic 🧄, tahini 🥄, salt 🧂, water 💧, cumin 🌿, and olive oil 🫗 in a food processor. Pulse until smooth
2. If too thick, add water 💧 one tablespoon at a time. Adjust seasoning
3. Transfer to a serving dish and drizzle with olive oil 🫗

## Notes
- Stores in the fridge for up to a week
- Garnish with parsley, paprika, or toasted pine nuts
